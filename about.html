About

My Story

Hi, my name is Justyna Bereza and I’m UI/UX Designer based in Germany, near Berlin.

I've been always passionate about creativity and continuous learning. Visual design is where I channel my creativity to craft compelling, user friendly and aesthetically pleasing designs. Whether it’s designing websites, user interfaces, logos, or marketing materials. I relish the opportunity to transform ideas into visually captivating representations.

Photography is another of my passions. I worked as a stage photographer for the world famous Swedish artists and many of my works were published on their official releases.

One of my hobbies is also photorealistic drawing. I find immense satisfaction in the meticulous process of creating drawings that appear almost like photographs. The level of detail and precision involved in this art form is both challenging and rewarding, and it allows me to express my admiration for realism.

But perhaps one of my greatest strengths is my unwavering enthusiasm for learning new things. I consider myself a perpetual student, always eager to explore new subjects, acquire new skills, and broaden my horizons. Whether it's diving into visual design, a new language, delving into the intricacies of a scientific field or mastering a new software tool, I find joy in the process of gaining knowledge and expertise.

I have also designed and I’ve being an admin of my personal and professional websites.

My hobbies are also travelling, singing, hiking, reading and cosplay. I love spending time with my cats. I love silence and like spending time on my own. I am also a thinker and I love keep my mind busy.
