QPay

Digital Wallet with Loyalty Program

Background

The Brief

Problem

Many digital wallets with loyalty program are overly complex and hard to set up and to use. Many lack a good security protection and are limited to one operating system excluding users on other platforms. Loyalty programs often aren’t enough engaging and are too confusing for the user.

Solution

Designing a digital wallet with loyalty program which is user friendly, intuitive and design with accessibility for both iOS and Android users. Clear design, engaging and easy to set up and use. Quick and secured transactions all over the world.

The Project Proposal

Objective

The app will simplify the payment process and make it easy by allowing users to link various payment methods and make quick and secure transactions in many different currencies. The aim is also to engage users by providing wide rage of personalised offers, promotions, and rewards tailored to individual preferences and activities in the app.

Key Features

  • linking bank accounts, credit cards, or other payment methods to the app for easy and secure digital payments without sharing financial data
  • earn rewards, points, or cashback by making transfers and engaging in loyalty program
  • personalized recommendations, promotions, and discounts based on user preferences
  • transaction and purchase history
  • security features such as fingerprint scanning and PIN codes
  • notifications

Competitor Research

PayPal

PayPal is a widely used online payment platform to make online payments quickly, easily and secured. It's widely accepted by many online retailers and service providers.

  • PayPal uses encryption and anti-fraud technology to protect users' financial information.
  • Setting up a PayPal account is straightforward, and the platform offers user-friendly interfaces.
  • PayPal operates in many countries and supports various currencies, making it suitable for international transactions.
  • PayPal offers mobile apps for iOS and Android
  • PayPal allows users to send money to friends and family members for free within the same country.
  • PayPal charges fees for receiving payments for goods and services, currency conversion, and cross-border payments.
  • Paypal although is very simple and intuitive to use doesn’t have a loyalty program and the mobile UI design seems a bit monotonous and old fasioned.

Payback

Payback is a loyalty program that allows also mobile payments and let user to earn points and rewards when making purchases from participating partner brands, and these points can often be redeemed for discounts or rewards.

  • PAYBACK members can earn points, vouchers and other rewards for their purchases providing a financial benefit.
  • The app offers partners with a wide range of businesses, giving members opportunities to earn rewards across different industries.
  • It creates personalised offers and discounts tailored to individual interests.
  • Joining PAYBACK is free of charge.
  • It offers mobile apps for iOS and Android and provides strong security features.
  • Can be a bit too complex for some users.
  • Expiration dates for earned rewards, and if customers don't use them in time, they can lose their value.
